About

The YoungArtists project aims to raise awareness of lesser-known artists from around the world. We want to help young talents to establish themselves and at the same time offer the audience the opportunity to discover starting artists. A side intention is for the community of up-and-coming creators to help each other through the trello web app, by creating what they enjoy for others.
